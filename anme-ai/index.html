What is Anmei AI?

Anme AI is an AI created by Xeno Gen Projects that focus on Realistic Figure to Anime Figure.

Based on Hinachi Ai

Anme AI also based on Hinachi Ai – CEL Engine but it doesn’t support a lot of features like Hinachi Ai but it does decent job on converting Realistic Figure to Anime Figure.

Diffusers and Renderers

Anme AI uses combination between diffuser and renderer in order to convert realistic image to an anime version.

Diffusers

How to get best images from Anme AI?

  • Give Anme AI non-blurry photo as possible if you are using AF 100. When you are using AFx 100 you don’t need a good picture because it will get easily detected and output the best quality image.

Do my photos get stored?

Your photo will get automatically deleted after Anme AI done converting them.
