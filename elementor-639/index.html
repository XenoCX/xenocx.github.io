Version

Target: Hinachi Ai (HAi)

Version: Hinachi Ai 3.0.2312

New

BATCH 1

  • Pose Editor – Hinachi Ai can now illustrate a specific pose with or without a reference. By openning Pose Editor, you can customized, give reference, or create a pose you want.
  • Server Base Requirements – In order to save space on our machine, Hinachi Ai will download diffusers, renderers, or any requirement in our server.  The users can has an option to save the contents downloaded or re-download it once it needs again this will save alot of disk space since Hinachi Ai takes alot of disk space.
  • Server Base Backup – Hinachi Ai now be backup in one push of a button. Rather than having a portable copy of Hinachi Ai. Portable Copy takes long to process and it takes alot of disk space. This update, reduce it by saving a stable version itself on our server. Can be restored by downloading your backup from our server.
